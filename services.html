What We Do Together

Design, Renovation & Styling

Every detail handled, every decision guided — so you can simply step into a home you love.

01

Turn-Key Renovations

Your home should grow with you. Whether it's a kitchen that has never worked quite right, a bathroom you've been meaning to address for years, or a whole-home transformation you've been building toward — Bonnie brings the vision, the contractor relationships, and the oversight to make it exceptional.

She manages the process start to finish — spatial planning, material selection, contractor communication, timeline management — so you are never juggling decisions alone. You step into the result.

Includes
  • Spatial planning & design direction
  • Material, tile, and fixture selection
  • Contractor coordination & oversight
  • Kitchen, bath, and whole-home scope
  • Final walk-through & project close
02

Full-Service Design

Building from the ground up or undertaking a significant remodel is one of the most personal things you'll ever do. Bonnie guides the creative direction from the very first blueprint to the final walk-through — every hard finish, every fixture, every architectural detail decided with intention.

The result is a home where the bones are as beautiful as they are functional. Where the decisions made at the beginning serve the family living there for decades.

Includes
  • Full design direction from blueprint stage
  • Hard finish, fixture & architectural detail selection
  • Mood boards & material presentations
  • Builder & trade coordination
  • New builds and major remodel scope
03

Room Styling & Furnishings

A room is finished not when there is nothing left to add, but when there is nothing left to remove. Bonnie curates furniture, textiles, rugs, and art that tell your family's story — not her preferences, yours.

Whether you are refreshing a single room or furnishing a newly built home from scratch, it culminates in a reveal day where every pillow is fluffed, every shelf is perfectly styled, and you fall in love with a space all over again.

Includes
  • Custom furniture sourcing & procurement
  • Textiles, rugs, and window treatments
  • Art curation & accessory styling
  • Single-room to whole-home scope
  • Full reveal day styling
04

Holiday & Hosting

Bonnie believes deeply in the power of gathering. A home that welcomes — that makes people want to linger, to come back, to feel held by the space — is one of the greatest gifts you can give your family and friends.

Her seasonal styling and holiday readiness services ensure your home is prepared to receive the people you love. You focus on the memories. She handles everything else.

Includes
  • Seasonal & holiday decor installation
  • Custom mantle garlands & tree styling
  • Thanksgiving & entertaining tablescapes
  • Front entry & exterior seasonal styling
  • Post-season takedown available
