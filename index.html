The Designer

Meet Bonnie

For thirty years, Bonnie Roden was the friend everyone called — the one who could walk into a home and see exactly what it was trying to become.

In 2024, she finally put her name on a door. Hearten Home Interiors is the business she spent three decades building without knowing it. Her goal is as simple as it is rare: to layer warmth, color, and texture so seamlessly that you love your home more. Not just because it's beautiful, but because it feels fundamentally yours.
