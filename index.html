Meet Bonnie

For thirty years, Bonnie Roden was the friend everyone called — the one who could walk into a home and see what it was trying to become.

In 2024, she finally put her name on a door. Hearten Home Interiors is the business she spent three decades building without knowing it — and her goal is as simple as it is rare: to make you love your home more. Not just because it's beautiful, but because it's finally, truly yours.
